# BabelChef Demo Video — Recording Script

## Setup

| Person | Device | Role |
|--------|--------|------|
| **Mom** | 💻 MacBook | Hindi speaker — in her living room in India, teaching the recipe |
| **Wife** | 📱 iPhone | English speaker — in the kitchen in the US, cooking |

Screen record the MacBook. One window per device. No tabs.

---

## Before Recording

### MacBook (Mom)
1. Open Chrome → BabelChef URL
2. Open QuickTime → New Screen Recording → select Internal Microphone

### iPhone (Wife — in kitchen)
1. Open Safari → BabelChef URL
2. Have some ingredients and utensils nearby

---

## THE SCRIPT

### 0:00 – 0:15 | Intro

▶️ **Start recording.**

**Mom (narrating in English):**

> "700 million multilingual families. No video call tool handles real-time translation. BabelChef does — powered by Gemini Live API."

---

### 0:15 – 0:40 | Connect the Call

**ON MACBOOK — Mom:**
1. Create Room → select **Hindi** → Create
2. Tell Wife the room code

**Mom says:** "I create a room as the Hindi speaker."

**ON IPHONE — Wife:**
1. Enter room code → select **English** → Join

**Mom says:** "My wife joins from her phone in the kitchen. We're connected."

**Screen shows both faces. Pause 3 seconds.**

---

### 0:40 – 1:20 | Wife Speaks English → Mom Hears Hindi

**Mom says:** "She's cooking and will ask me a question in English."

**WIFE says (from kitchen):**
> "Mom, the onions are golden brown. Should I add spices now?"

**ON MACBOOK:** Hindi audio plays. Subtitles show English + Hindi.

**Mom says:** "I heard her in Hindi. Subtitles show both languages — that's audio transcription."

**WIFE says excitedly:**
> "Oh my god this smells amazing!"

**Mom says:** "Her excitement carries through to Hindi. That's Affective Dialog."

---

### 1:20 – 2:00 | Mom Speaks Hindi → Wife Hears English (THE PROOF)

**Mom says:** "Now I'll guide her in Hindi."

**MOM speaks Hindi:**
> "हाँ बेटा, अब मसाले डाल दो। तेज़ आंच पर दो मिनट भून लो।"

**ON MACBOOK:** English subtitle appears.

**Mom says:** "I said 'add the spices, stir-fry two minutes on high heat.' Read the English subtitle — it matches perfectly."

**MOM speaks Hindi warmly:**
> "बहुत अच्छा कर रही हो! बिल्कुल सही बना रही हो।"

**Mom says:** "I said 'you're doing great, making it perfectly.' Judges — read the subtitle, it's spot on."

---

### 2:00 – 2:30 | Kitchen Noise (Wife's Side)

**Mom says:** "My wife is in a real kitchen. Watch what happens with noise."

**WIFE makes noise near her iPhone:**
- Clatters utensils
- Taps counter with spoon
- Runs faucet

**ON MACBOOK:** Nothing. No phantom Hindi translations appear.

**Mom says:** "She's clattering pans, running water. I hear nothing — no phantom translations. Proactive Audio filters kitchen noise."

**WIFE then speaks clearly:**
> "Mom, how much salt should I add?"

**ON MACBOOK:** Hindi translation appears instantly.

**Mom says:** "But when she speaks — instant translation."

---

### 2:30 – 2:50 | Cooking Tools

**Mom says:** "BabelChef has Cooking Tools via function calling."

**WIFE says:** "How many grams is 2 cups of flour?"

**Wait for response.**

**Mom says:** "Measurement conversion mid-call."

**WIFE says:** "What does tadka mean?"

**Mom says:** "Cultural cooking terms explained — recipes need understanding, not just translation."

---

### 2:50 – 3:10 | Architecture

**Mom says:** "Three ADK agents on Google Cloud Run. Two translation agents on gemini-live-2.5-flash-native-audio for bidirectional audio. One cooking assistant on gemini-2.0-flash-live. All on Vertex AI, deployed live."

---

### 3:10 – 3:25 | Closing

**Mom says:** "BabelChef sees, hears, and speaks — making cross-cultural cooking feel like you're in the same kitchen. Built with Gemini Live API, ADK, and Google Cloud for the Gemini Live Agent Challenge. Open source on GitHub. Thank you."

⏹️ **Stop recording.**
