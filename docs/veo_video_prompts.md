## VEO Tips

- Generate each scene separately as a 5-8s clip
- Use **16:9 aspect ratio** for YouTube
- Add voiceover narration from your demo script afterward
- Add text overlays in iMovie, CapCut, or DaVinci Resolve (free)
- Stitch scenes together with 0.5s crossfade transitions
- Total video: ~45-60 seconds (promotional) 
- **Pair this with your live screen recording** for the full submission
